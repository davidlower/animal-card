Siberian Husky

Interesting fact: Huskies have multiple layers of fur.

  • Diet: Fish, seal.
  • Exercise: A lot.
  • Location: Siberia & Alaska.
  • Coat: Double fur coat.

Huskies are great pack animals and can run for miles without loosing too much engery. They can survive in temperatures below -50C. Their howl can be heard up to 10 miles away.
